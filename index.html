Missile Car

Missile Car game is all about the car controlling and shooting out the missile to destroy the car that's come from opposite direction. Thus you get coins to destroy the cars and unlock the new vehicles, tracks, and missile packs.

Tilt your mobile to move your vehicle horizontally and it will move as your tilting direction. Touch in the game screen to shoot out the missiles and destroy the cars which will come from opposite direction. Engage your car with missile pack and thus you get your missile reloaded. Get the coins by engaging you car with coin and unlock the new features. You can also earn coins by watching videos.


App Features

  • 8 cars and 2 bikes
  • 3 missile packs
  • 3 tracks
  • Coin earning options

Used technologies/libraries/etc

  • Unity Game Engine and C# programming language
  • Touch phase and count to shoot missile
  • Acceleration to move car left and right
  • Rigidbody 2D used to control velocity of cars
  • Box collider to detect collision between objects
  • Scene managers to load scenes
  • Shared Preferences to store scores and setting options
  • Sharing intent to share game
