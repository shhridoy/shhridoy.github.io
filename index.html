Shahi Dua & Zikir (সহীহ দোআ ও যিকির)
Version 1.0

Shahi Dua & Zikir (সহীহ দোআ ও যিকির) is an android app that is originally created from a book called Hisnul Muslim was written by Dr. Saeed ibn Ali ibn al-Qahtani and translated in Bengali by Abu Bakar Muhammad Zakaria. In this app, there are a lot of Dua and Zikirs included by their references from Holy Quran and Hadith.

App Topics

  • Purification and Prayer (পবিত্রতা ও সালাত)
  • Anxiety, disaster, fear, disease, and death (দুশ্চিন্তা, মুসীবত, ভয়, রোগ ও মৃত্যু)
  • Daily and social way of life (দৈনন্দিন ও সামাজিক জীবনাচার)
  • Marriage and conjugal Topics (বিবাহ ও দাম্পত্য বিষয়াবলী)
  • Remembrance of the Qur'an and the Sunnah supplication (কুরআন সুন্নাহর যিকির ও দুআ)
  • Faith Protection (ঈমান সুরক্ষা)
  • Happy & ominous signs (শুভ অশুভ লক্ষন)
  • Movement, tour (চলাচল, সফর)
  • Hajj and Umrah (হজ্জ্ব ও উমরা)
  • Fasting for Ramadan (সাওম [রোজা])
  • Sleep and miscellaneous (ঘুম ও বিবিধ)

App Features

  • Catagorized topics.
  • User can save the important dua & zikirs on bookmarks.
  • User can also share the content on Social Media.
  • Facility to choose the app theme as user want.
  • App font size and face also can be changed.

Used technologies/libraries/etc

  • NavigationView & DrawerLayout
  • Fragments to view contents
  • SQLite Database to save contents
  • Shared Preferences to change app theme, font size & font color as well
  • Sharing intent to share contents

Missile Car
Version 1.1

Missile Car game is all about the car controlling and shooting out the missile to destroy the car that's come from opposite direction. Thus you get coins to destroy the cars and unlock the new vehicles, tracks, and missile packs.

Tilt your mobile to move your vehicle horizontally and it will move as your tilting direction. Touch in the game screen to shoot out the missiles and destroy the cars which will come from opposite direction. Engage your car with missile pack and thus you get your missile reloaded. Get the coins by engaging you car with coin and unlock the new features. You can also earn coins by watching videos.


App Features

  • 8 cars and 2 bikes
  • 3 missile packs
  • 3 tracks
  • Coin earning options

Used technologies/libraries/etc

  • Unity Game Engine and C# programming language
  • Touch phase and count to shoot missile
  • Acceleration to move car left and right
  • Rigidbody 2D used to control velocity of cars
  • Box collider to detect collision between objects
  • Scene managers to load scenes
  • Shared Preferences to store scores and setting options
  • Sharing intent to share game
